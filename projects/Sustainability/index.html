Sustainability 101

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

These three R's are most commonly known. Did you know that there are actually 5 R's total?
They are, in order:

  • Refuse
  • Reduce
  • Reuse
  • Recycle
  • Rot
Learn more about how you can make use of the 5 R's to help you, your community, and the environment below.
